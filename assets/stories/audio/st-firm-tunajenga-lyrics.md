# Tunajenga — Ravine to the World

Original ST-Firm anthem production sheet  
Tempo: 113 BPM  
Lead: female mezzo/alto  
Style: Amapiano × Gengetone × cinematic future-Africa

## Performance principles

- The female lead owns the record; she begins intimately, becomes rhythmically confident, and closes emotionally.
- Kiswahili pronunciation must be natural and unforced.
- The male voice appears only for the ST-Firm studio introduction and founder announcement.
- The community chorus must sound human and celebratory, not like a stadium effect.
- Do not imitate Bien, Alikiba, or any other identifiable performer.
- Do not copy the melody, rhythm, chant, instrumental or recording of any reference song.
