# Tunajenga cover generation record

## Mode

OpenAI built-in image generation, followed by deterministic local composition for exact logos, flags and typography.

## Base background prompt

Create a square premium music-cover background for an East African future anthem. Show a cinematic, bright African-futurist journey from Eldama Ravine in Kenya to Berlin, Deutschland. Use luminous Kenyan highlands and coffee plants in the foreground, a subtle coastal runway and passenger aircraft, and a clean futuristic light path flowing toward Berlin. Include one symbolic young Kenyan male engineer, not a real identifiable person, seen from behind holding a slim laptop case. Use premium photorealistic cinematic key art with tasteful 3D lighting. Preserve generous negative space for title and artist overlays. Use emerald green, white, electric purple, restrained warm gold and natural sky blue. Do not include words, typography, logos, emblems, flags, watermarks, currency, coins, extra people, a dark background or an oversized aircraft.

## Targeted correction

Replace only the destination skyline with an unmistakably authentic Berlin, Deutschland cityscape: a plausible Fernsehturm at Alexanderplatz, restrained European mid-rise architecture and a subtle Brandenburg Gate silhouette. Remove any Asian-megacity appearance, Shanghai-style skyline, Oriental Pearl Tower or supertall tower cluster. Preserve the engineer, coffee plants, runway, airplane, glowing route, bright sunrise, green landscape, negative space, palette and camera angle. Add no text, logos, flags or watermark.

## Deterministic overlay

The final 3000 × 3000 composition is produced by `scripts/build-tunajenga-cover.ps1` using the real ST-Firm, SSOS and Akädemie assets, programmatically constructed Kenya and Deutschland flags, and exact release typography.
