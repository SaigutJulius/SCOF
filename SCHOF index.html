What is SCOF?

SCOF (Saigut Coffee Token) is a Real-World Asset (RWA) backed digital token representing the future of Kenyan coffee production, export, machines, and restaurant integration. It is the first Kenya–Germany dual-market coffee blockchain ecosystem designed to empower investors, farmers, machinery importers, and hospitality venues.
